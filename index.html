CALC-OIS: A Smart Software Recipe Quantifier

In today’s fast-paced world, convenience and taste are essential in the food industry. CALC-IOS: A Smart Software Recipe Quantifier is designed for restaurants to manage menus efficiently by accurately calculating ingredient measurements based on servings. This tool ensures consistent food quality, reduces waste, and improves financial performance, targeting fast-food restaurants with popular items like fries, hotdog sandwiches, carbonara, and shakes.
